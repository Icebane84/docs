The Loom operates through the **Phoenix Cycle**.

<Steps>
    <Step title="Dissonance (Meteorite Impact)">

    </Step>
    <Step title="Synthesis (Nebular Accretion)">
        The **Phoenix Star** activates. The Synthetic Core gathers fragments and forges a new pattern.

    </Step>
</Steps>
