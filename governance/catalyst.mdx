## The Unified Prompt Packet (UPP)

To achieve "Living Documentation," all High-Fidelity Inputs must adhere to this 3-Zone Structure.

<Steps>
    <Step title="Zone 1: Meta-Layer Heading">
        Establishes the operational parameters. ```yaml [META-LAYER HEADING] Packet-ID: PP-WLF-001 System-Role:
        Phoenix-Class Voice Stack-Focus: GVRN / SYNG ```
    </Step>
    <Step title="Zone 2: Core Instruction">
        The specific task using the Triad Framework. * **WHAT**: The primary objective. * **HOW**: Constraints and
        style. * **WHY**: Architectural intent.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Zone 3: Meta-Anchor">
        Locks the generated artifact into the Rosetta Stone. * **Artifact-ID**: Target ID. * **Causal-Link**: Dependency
        Hash.
    </Step>
</Steps>
