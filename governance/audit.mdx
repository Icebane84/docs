## The v13.0 Checklist (The Crystal Gate)

Every artifact must clear these internal checks before canonization.

<Steps>
    <Step title="UIP Integrity">Is the 12-point **Vector Signature** present and populated?</Step>
    <Step title="Nomenclature (RNC)">Does the ID follow `DOMAIN.Subsystem.Descriptor`?</Step>
    <Step title="AGP Integration">Are **State Vector** and **Risk Governance** blocks present?</Step>
    <Step title="Relational Weave">Does it contain a **Standardized Synergy Block**?</Step>
</Steps>

## Violation & Reclamation

If an artifact fails the audit:

1.  **MARK**: Tag header as `[FAILED_AUDIT]`.
2.  **FLAG**: Inject `> [!CAUTION]` alert.
3.  **REFORGE**: Return to `GVRN.Pipe.Operational` for transmutation.
