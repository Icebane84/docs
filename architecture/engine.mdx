## The Phoenix Cycle (Systemic Alchemy)

The Engine operates via a continuous evolutionary loop:

<Steps>
    <Step title="Dissonance (The Call)">
        The **Coherence Attractor Core** detects a logical or thematic gap (Dissonance Signature).
    </Step>
    <Step title="Synthesis (The Forge)">
        The Engine spins up a **Context Weave** to resolve the gap, generating new insights.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Transcendence (The Rebirth)">
        The resolved insight is canonized, permanently upgrading the System State.
    </Step>
</Steps>
